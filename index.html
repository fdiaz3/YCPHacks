What's a Hackathon?

An overnight event (usually over 24 hours) at a college where you work together with a team to make something awesome. You don't need to know how to code or be a supreme hacker. Just how to be creative!

Where is this going to be?

Specifically, the main ballroom of The Yorktowne Hotel! Broadly, in York, PA. There will be free parking across the street for the whole weekend.

When will this be?

It will be held Friday October 21 to Sunday October 23 2016

Why

Learn new things, meet new people, and tons of free food and swag.

Will you reimburse travel?

Sadly this year we cannot reimburse travel.

Can I come?

We'll take any college student from anywhere on earth!

I don't have a team so....

Well you're in luck! We have tons of team building activities if you don't have a team to come with you.

I can't code

We'll have tons of great workshops and mentors to help you learn and build something amazing!

How much does it cost?

Zip. Nada. Gratis. Free. Everything including food, parking, getting in, and swag. Seriously why aren't you applying right now?

What should I bring?

Clothing, sleeping bag, any necessary tech (and chargers), and deodorant.
